Integrated Economics

CREATE is a deflationary token that will be the native currency on our marketplace to be used as transaction fees

How to Buy $CREATE

Create Your Wallet

Download Metamask and create a wallet if you do not already have one

Write down your seed phrase on a piece of paper and store in a secure place (Never share your seed phrase or enter it into any site, other than MetaMask.io)

Add Binance Smart Chain Network

Navigate to "Settings"    (       icon on mobile)

Click "Networks"

Click "Add Network" and fill in the details as shown in the picture below

Save and navigate back to your wallet. It should now display BNB and "Smart Chain" as your network.

Send BNB to your wallet

Purchase BNB on your preferred exchange and send it to your wallet that is connected to the Smart Chain

(Be sure to use BEP20 network when sending)

Swap BNB For CREATE on PancakeSwap

Navigate to "Browser" within your wallet

Go to exchange.pancakeswap.finance and connect your wallet

Click on "Select a currency", paste the contract address, and then click "import"

Contract Address:   0x057a0a5b701f50fd47694c1cfba427c5a6fd264a

Click on transaction settings and set the slippage to 12%

Select the amount of BNB you'd like use and confirm the transaction to swap for CREATE
